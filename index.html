Architectural BIM Services

We create detailed architectural 3D models from 2D sketches or CAD drawings. Our models include all elements like walls, floors, ceilings, doors, windows, and interior details according to LOD 100 to LOD 500 standards.

  • Schematic Design Modeling
  • Construction Documentation
  • Interior Modeling
  • Quantity Take-offs

Structural BIM Services

Our structural BIM modeling services involve creating detailed 3D models of structural components including steel, concrete, and timber structures. We focus on accuracy to ensure seamless fabrication and construction.

  • Reinforcement Detailing
  • Steel Structure Modeling
  • Precast Concrete Modeling
  • Structural Analysis Support
