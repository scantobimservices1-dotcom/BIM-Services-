Architectural BIM Services

We transform 2D conceptual sketches or CAD drawings into highly detailed 3D architectural models. Our expertise covers the entire project lifecycle, from schematic design to construction documentation, ensuring every architectural detail—from walls and partitions to complex ceiling systems—is precisely represented according to global LOD 100 to LOD 500 standards.

Detailed Floor Plans & Sections
Exterior & Interior 3D Modeling

Structural BIM Services

Our structural BIM modeling services involve creating intelligent 3D models of structural components, including steel, reinforced concrete, and timber. We prioritize accuracy to ensure that the structural design aligns perfectly with architectural intent, facilitating seamless fabrication and construction onsite.

Rebar Detailing & 3D Modeling
Steel Connection Detailing
Precast Concrete Modeling
Structural Quantity Take-offs

MEP BIM Services (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing)

We specialize in modeling complex Mechanical (HVAC), Electrical, Plumbing, and Fire Protection systems. Our MEP models are designed for constructability, ensuring that systems are routed efficiently and integrated without conflict into the building's architecture and structure.

  • HVAC Ducting & Piping Modeling
  • Electrical Conduit & Cable Tray Routing
  • Sanitary & Water Supply Systems
  • Fire Protection & Sprinkler Modeling

BIM Coordination & Clash Detection

Identify and resolve interferences before construction begins. Using Navisworks, we perform automated clash reports between Architectural, Structural, and MEP disciplines to eliminate costly field rework and scheduling delays.

Hard Clash Detection

Identifying physical intersections between different building components like pipes hitting beams.

Soft Clash (Clearance)

Ensuring equipment has enough operational and maintenance space according to codes.

Scan to BIM Services

We convert Point Cloud Data from laser scans into precise, as-built 3D BIM models. This is ideal for renovation, retrofit, or facility management projects where original blueprints may be missing or inaccurate.

Point Cloud Processing As-Built Documentation Historical Renovation
